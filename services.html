Common questions

FAQ

Do you work with clients outside the UK?
Yes. The practice is remote-first and works with clients across Europe and North America. All project communication happens asynchronously by default, with video calls scheduled when needed.
How long does a typical project take?
Foundation engagements typically run 4–6 weeks. Standard projects run 8–12 weeks depending on scope and content availability. Timelines are agreed in writing before any work begins.
Do you work with existing WordPress sites?
Sometimes, depending on the existing setup. Most engagements start from a clean base or involve migrating content into a new block theme. A brief discovery call will clarify whether your existing site is a good starting point.
What do you need from clients to get started?
A clear sense of what you're trying to accomplish, existing brand assets if they exist, and enough content to make layout decisions. No design skills or technical knowledge required on your side.
Can you maintain the site after launch?
The Standard project deliverable includes a codebase and documentation designed for independent maintenance. If ongoing support is needed, a monthly retainer can be arranged at the outset.
